Who is Kurama?

Kurama, also known as the Nine-Tailed Fox, is one of the most powerful Tailed Beasts in the Naruto universe. Feared for centuries, Kurama forms a unique bond with Naruto Uzumaki.

Abilities

  • Massive Chakra Reserves
  • Tailed Beast Bomb
  • Enhanced Regeneration
  • Negative Emotion Sensing

Did You Know?

Kurama was originally sealed inside Naruto at birth and later became one of his greatest allies.
